The Digital Anonymous Citizen

AmityCoin is a project dedicated to using protocol and code to empower the digital anonymous citizen. Our focus is on privacy, anonymity, security and in the long-term, quantum resistance.

Article 12 of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights:
